MULTI-TISSUE INTEGRATION
========================================================================
Method: Stouffer's weighted Z-meta analysis
  Z_combined = sum(w_i * Z_i) / sqrt(sum(w_i^2))
  where w_i = sqrt(N_i) per tissue

Tissues integrated: Nerve_Tibial (N=532) + Whole_Blood (N=670)
Software: Custom Python script (sample_weighted_stouffer.py)
Output: GTEx multi-tissue integrated Z-scores per gene × phenotype
Sensitivity: ACAT-O method (validates against Stouffer results)
