Method:

META-ANALYSIS (DR)
========================================================================

Studies included:
  1. FinnGen R13 DR (GTEx weights)  — baseline
  2. UK Biobank DR subset 1 (eQTLGen weights)
  3. UK Biobank DR subset 2 (eQTLGen weights)
  4. Cai et al. 2026 UKB T2D-DR

Gene: RNH1 (lead cross-complication candidate)

Heterogeneity assessment:
  - Cochran's Q = 40.0
  - I² = 95.0% (substantial heterogeneity)
  - P_heterogeneity < 0.0001
  - FE Z = +3.37 (P = 7.4 × 10⁻⁴)
  - 95% prediction interval: [-4.00, +10.68] (crosses zero)

Interpretation:
  - FE meta-analysis marginally significant
  - I²=95% indicates extreme heterogeneity
  - Wide 95% PI crossing zero → the effect cannot be reliably predicted
    in a new study
  - Cross-population replication fails
